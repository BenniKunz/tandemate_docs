Privacy Policy for TandeMate

Effective Date: 30/03/2026

At TandeMate, we are dedicated to safeguarding the privacy and confidentiality of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile app, TandeMate, and its services.

1. Data We Collect & How We Use It

  1. Precise Location Data:
    TandeMate requests access to your device's specific location to help you find nearby language partners.
    Storage: This data is stored securely using Google Firebase.
    Visibility: Other users of the app will be able to see your approximate distance (e.g., "500m away") to facilitate meetups. We do not share your exact GPS coordinates with other users.
    No Background Tracking: We only access your location while the app is in use.
  2. Account & Profile Information: We collect your name, email, and language goals to create your discovery profile.
  3. Technical Log Data: We use Google Crashlytics to collect information about app crashes and performance issues. This may include device type, OS version, and certain identifiers that help us fix bugs, but it does not include your name or private messages.

2. Third-Party Data Processors

To provide our services, we share certain data with the following third-party providers:

  • Google Maps SDK: Used to provide mapping and location services. Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure cloud storage of your profile and location data. Privacy Details.
  • Google Crashlytics: Used to monitor app stability and fix technical errors.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when you allow location permissions on your device.
  • Contractual Necessity: We cannot show you "nearby" partners without processing your location data.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your location and profile data as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time within the app settings, which will trigger the deletion of your personal data from our Firebase database within [Insert Days, e.g., 30] days.

5. Security

We leverage Firebase's industry-standard security protocols to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
